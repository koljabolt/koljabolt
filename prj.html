Berlin Kolja is currently expanding into game development — learning code (C#, Python, GDScript), working with Godot and Unity, and studying game design from the ground up. Writing narratives, designing characters, building prototypes. In other words telling stories.

K. moved to Berlin and studied at Catalyst Film Institute. He directed several short films, storyboarded extensively — for his own projects and for other students. 2017 – 2022 STORYBOARD ARTIST

Five years across three studios, most of them at Rocket Fox — where Kolja developed storyboards for Captain Kraken and Nibble Nom, both YouTube animated series. He worked in the writers' room, recorded voice actors, boarded musical numbers, fight scenes, chase sequences, gag scenes. Led a small project: an animated music video for Freezepop. Closed out the period at MetraFilms, adding Premiere Pro to the toolkit alongside Adobe Animate, Toon Boom Harmony, Storyboard Pro, and Adobe Audition.
